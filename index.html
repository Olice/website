Driven by design, our aim is to bring all the benefits of digital media to your e-learning content.

Who is Olice?

Since 2006 we have been providing our clients with a variety of multimedia solutions, including e-learning and training materials, high production-value video content and mobile applications. Our client portfolio includes

  • Ovo Energy
  • Las Iguanas
  • Scimed Consulting
  • Nokia
  • Otsuka

Our work has been translated into a number of different languages and is in everyday use around the globe.
